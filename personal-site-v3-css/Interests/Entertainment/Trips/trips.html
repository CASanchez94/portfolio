My parents were both extremely busy due to their jobs and somehow managed to both get their vacation time approved. So, one thing my family always wanted to do was go see the San Antonio River Walk during Christmas because it's such a beautiful sight. I just remember getting there and roaming the River Walk with my sister and enjoying the beautiful lights the city put up. It made me think at the time that life isn't so bad and i should continue moving forward.

Activities

  • Walked along the River Walk with my family.
  • Visited the Alamo for its historical significance because no matter how many times you visit, it never really gets old.
  • Visited the San Antonio Zoo because we wanted to see how it compared to Gladys Porter Zoo.
