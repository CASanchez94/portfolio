Captain America: Civil War

Alright, I know what you're thinking: a superhero movie, and a Marvel one at that? But in my honest and humble opinion, Civil War is by far the most peak movie in the MCU. This movie has it all—world building, real-world consequences, and action. For example, Steve Rogers and Tony Stark’s relationship has always been kind of strained, but when Stark finds out that Bucky killed his parents… I am Team Captain, but RDJ did such a good job showing the pain he felt, which led to the most emotional scene of Rogers and Stark fighting and ultimately the implosion of The Avengers.
